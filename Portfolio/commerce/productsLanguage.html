Language

Dictionary

Necessary for anyone that is planning to travel to a country that speaks a foregin language. This dictionary is small enough to fit into a backpack or even your back pocket. If you are ever attempting to speak to someone but can't think of the word to use, use your dictionary! Available translation for 34 languages.


Common Words

This book is excellent for a quick search on the most common words used in each language. With up to 35 languages available, this book will allow you to quickly look up the most used words in each country, such as "bathroom", "thank you", "excuse me", and of course "one beer please"


Language Learning

Available for every language spoken on the planet, this book will take you all the way from the very beginnig of learning a new language to the expert level. It will encourage you to practice at least 10 minutes for 5 days a week in order to get to that expert level of language. Very useful for those that wish to learn a language well before travelling.


Survival Guide

Once again available in many different languages, this book allows detailed explanations for how to survive visiting a city that speaks a language that you don't understand! Best used when studied a week or two before your trip, this book explains key words, culture differences, and appropriate clothing.


Travel Tips

This collection contains a book representing every country on the planet! Read through this book before your travels in order to be fully and completely informed on safety, culture, tourist attractions, hidden gems, and language tips. It will even give suggestions on what types of foods to try and which
