Clothing

Winter Jacket

Essential for travelling in the northern regions during the winter. Keep yourself warm and protected during the frigid winter temperatures. Also very useful for any trips to Antartica.


Scarf

Look fashionable no matter where you travel. Especially useful for breweries and soccer games (although you will likely have to call it "football". Also useful to put on your head to hide your hair for some countries! Look good while still respecting a culture's customs.


Flip Flops

Essential if you plan to travel to a tropical climate. Seen everywhere among the locals, these easy and light shoes are necessary for the complete beach, tropical, or boat experience. Come in many colors and designs. Send me an e-mail if you have a specific design in mind that you would like to be custom made.


Cowboy Boots

Seen in nearly every small American town in the midwest, and in every city in the south, the cowboy boot may not be used by ranchers anymore, but the city folk still use them! You won't stand out for a minute in most of America, but you might still get a side glance in Chicago!


Jungle Jacket

Brazil, Vietnam, Laos, Cambodia, or Thailand. No matter what country you're in, if you plan on entering the unpredicable jungle, you had best be prepared for whatever awaits. This comfortable jacket allows for room to hold a number of tools, accessories, or survival kits for your next advanture.
